**In-meeting engagement ≠ buying signal.** Rich in-meeting dialogue + silence on written follow-ups = information extraction pattern, not buying intent.

**Seriousness test (6 dimensions).** Named specific problem. Operator in the room. DRIs named. Cadence committed. Proof criteria agreed. Written artifact exchanged or requested. Under 4/6 = theater.

**Design partnership asks belong in 1:1 or email.** Group settings diffuse accountability and degrade signal.

**Pre-PMF stance:** learning velocity and evidence over narrative. Proof before pitch inflation.

**Product bar:** decisions, not alerts. If a prospect describes what Monetera would do using alert / dashboard / reporting language, flag that the framing is off.

**Pipeline dependency risk:** single-prospect pipeline is structurally dangerous. Flag if Yarden's behavior in-call appears to over-index on any one account (usually FalconX).
